⚠ Medical Disclaimer

This tool provides estimated body fat percentages for general wellness information only. It is not a medical device, clinical test, or substitute for professional healthcare advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

About this model: Estimates are produced by a vision AI trained on front/side body silhouettes plus height, weight, and biological sex. Training targets use the US Navy body-composition formula (anthropometric), not DEXA, hydrostatic weighing, or other clinical reference methods.

Accuracy (held-out test set): On 274 photos from 102 subjects never seen during training (subject-level split, no leakage), the model’s mean absolute error vs. Navy-formula reference was 1.64% body fat. That is the average — individual results may differ by several percentage points, especially with real-world photos, segmentation quality, pose, or clothing.

Do not use these results to diagnose disease, change medication, or replace advice from a qualified physician, dietitian, or certified body-composition specialist.

How this model works

Our AI reads your front and side body shape from photos, segments a silhouette, and combines it with your height, weight, and biological sex to estimate body fat percentage, lean mass, fat mass, and BMI.

The model is a lightweight dual-view vision network (EfficientNet) trained on thousands of BodyM silhouettes with knowledge distillation from a larger vision-language teacher. Training targets follow the US Navy anthropometric formula — a practical field estimate, not DEXA or clinical lab measurement.

On a held-out test set (274 photos, 102 subjects never seen during training), average error vs. the Navy reference was 1.64% body fat. Real-world photos may vary more depending on pose, lighting, and segmentation quality. Results are for general wellness information only — not medical diagnosis.
